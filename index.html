The Foundation

Every Member Agrees to Three Laws

Violation of any of these three laws results in immediate removal from the Alliance.

I

Use AI Legally

No automation of illegal activity. No facilitation of fraud. No use of AI to deceive, manipulate, or harm.

II

Use AI Ethically

Respect human dignity, privacy, and autonomy. Be honest about what AI does and does not do.

III

Use AI Morally

Build systems that make humans stronger, not dependent. Create value for all stakeholders.

"Strong humans build smart systems. Smart systems do not replace strong humans. They extend them." — The Human Principle, Block by Block AI Safety & Security Alliance
The Framework

Three Conversations. Every Engagement Starts Here.

Organizations that fail at AI governance skip step one. Never skip to Operations.

01

Governance "What are you allowed to do?"

  • AI Acceptable Use Policy
  • Vendor approval process
  • Data permissions & classification
  • Regulatory requirements mapping
02

Enablement "Are your people trained?"

  • AI literacy assessment
  • Training plan development
  • Adoption metrics & tracking
  • Certification pathway
03

Operations "Which workflows give the highest ROI?"

  • Map top 3 processes for AI integration
  • Design AI-assisted workflows
  • Implement one at a time
